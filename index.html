We're getting married!

We're thrilled to invite you to celebrate with us from January 8-15th, 2025!
We can't wait to share this special moment with you and would be honored to have you be a part of our joyous occasion.

Countdown To Our Wedding!

Events

January 9th

Welcome Party & Mehndi 7PM - Outdoors

Chalo, time to kick off the celebrations! Join us for a chilled-out welcome party, all set outdoors. Grab a drink, catch up with everyone, and enjoy some music while the mehndi artists get to work. The bride and all the ladies can get their mehndi done and start the wedding feels. It's going to be a fun, laid-back night to relax, laugh, and gear up for the big days ahead!

January 10th

Ladies Sangeet & Jaggo 7PM - Indoors

Get ready for a night full of dholki beats and non-stop dancing at the Ladies Sangeet! Bring all your energy because it's going to be a proper Punjabi night indoors, with everyone showing off their best moves. We'll also be doing the Jaggo— so get ready to light up the night and bring that wedding spirit! Sing your hearts out, dance like no one's watching, and let's make this one to remember.

January 11th

Maiyaan & Choora Ceremony 4PM - Outdoors

Time for some pre-wedding rituals! Join us outdoors for the Maiyaan and Choora Ceremony, where we shower Rami and Rabia with love and blessings. During the Maiyaan, turmeric paste will be applied to both the bride and groom to get them glowing and ready for the big day. After that, Rabia will have her Choora ceremony, where she'll receive her traditional bangles to mark the start of her new journey. It's going to be a beautiful, emotional moment, full of family and tradition— don't miss it!

January 12th

Anand Karaj 10AM - Outdoors

The big moment is here— join us for the Anand Karaj, the wedding ceremony, set outdoors. Under the warmth of the sun and surrounded by nature, the bride and groom will take their sacred vows in front of Guru Granth Sahib Ji. It's a peaceful, spiritual ceremony where love and commitment are celebrated as they walk around the Guru four times. Come be part of this beautiful moment and bless the couple as they start their journey together!

Lunch 1PM - Indoors

Join us for a delicious lunch right after the wedding ceremony. Celebrate with us as we share some tasty food and good company. It's a perfect time to relax and enjoy the rest of the day together!

January 13th

Cocktail Hour 7PM - Indoors

Get ready to mix and mingle during our Cocktail Hour! Enjoy a variety of drinks and tasty appetizers while catching up with friends and family. It's the perfect way to unwind and celebrate before the evening festivities kick off. Cheers!

Reception 8PM - Indoors

Join us for the grand finale of the wedding at the Reception! Let's celebrate the newlyweds with great food, lively music, and lots of dancing. It's the last event of this amazing celebration, so bring your energy and let's make some unforgettable memories together. Can't wait to see you on the dance floor!
